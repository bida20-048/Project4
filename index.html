About

BuffClan Capital Group is a Botswana-based financial services conglomerate with an expansive vision for Africa. Positioned as a trusted partner for businesses, governments, and investors, BuffClan Capital is committed to reshaping the financial services landscape across the continent by fostering sustainable growth, innovation, and collaboration. Through a broad range of specialized services, we provide solutions designed to address the unique economic, environmental, and social challenges of the African market

Mission

Provide creative, dependable, and dynamic financial services that cater to the particular opportunities and problems faced by African clients in order to empower and support them.

Values

Respect and Integrity

Guided by the highest ethical standards, we prioritize transparency and respect, fostering trust among clients and partners.
